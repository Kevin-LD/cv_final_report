\begin{abstract}
This report presents two computer vision and embodied AI tasks.

The first task focuses on neural rendering and AIGC-based 3D reconstruction and generation, including real-world multi-view reconstruction, text-to-3D generation, single-image-to-3D generation, and scene fusion rendering. COLMAP, 2D Gaussian Splatting (2DGS), and the pre-trained DREAMFUSION-IF and Magic123 frameworks in threestudio are utilized to construct a complete 3D vision pipeline.

\end{abstract}
